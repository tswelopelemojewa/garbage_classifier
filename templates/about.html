ABOUT OUR APPLICATION




Our innovative waste classification model harnesses the power of cutting-edge machine learning technologies to revolutionize waste management. By leveraging advanced algorithms and image recognition capabilities, our application can accurately categorize waste into six distinct classes: metal, glass, plastic, paper, steel, and trash. This solution not only streamlines the sorting process in recycling facilities but also promotes sustainability by ensuring that each material is properly allocated for recycling or disposal. With our system, we aim to contribute to a greener and more efficient approach to waste management, reducing environmental impact and conserving valuable resources.
